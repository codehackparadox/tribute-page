Frank James Lampard

One of the legendary footballer in England


Here's a timeline of Frank Lampard's life:


  • 1978 - born in Romford, London, England

  • 1995 - began his career as a professional footballer at West Ham United

  • 1999 - debut to England National team (106 Caps, 29 Goals, 9 Penalties)

  • 2001 - joined Chelsea for $15m (211 Goals, 102 Assists, 3 EPL, 1 UEFA CL, 1 UEFA EL, 4 FA Cups, 2 Football League Cups)

  • 2005 - named FWA Footballer of the year, Fifa World Player of the year and runner-up for Ballon d'Or

  • 2014 - signed to New York City club and loaned to Manchester City for 6-months deal ended in Januray 2015 which was then extended

  • 2015 - awarded an Order of British Empire

  • 2015-2017 - team captain on ITV Sports Panel show "Play to the Whistle"

  • 2018 - started his coaching career at Derby County

  • 2019 - signed as Chelsea's head coach and was dismissed after 18 months


"I am certainly not the most talented player in the world; there's many a more talented player than me."

-- Frank Lampard
